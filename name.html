Update of Company Name

Platinum Programming is now Egnition Labratories!

Written by Rowan on June 24, 2020.

Last Saturday, some of you may have noticed a change in the naming on some pages, and noticed the logo has changed from the Platinum Programming logo to a foreign logo that is an "E" combined with an "L".

Well, we folks here at Egnition Labratories have decided to expand the company beyond just programming, and to go along with that, we decided to change the name.

Also, Platinum Programming, even though it is alliterative, it doesn't really fit with the company. Egnition Labs is much better, in my opinion.
